3. Trading Strategies & Risk Management

⚠️ Risk Warning

Trading cryptocurrencies involves substantial risk of loss. Never trade with money you cannot afford to lose.

Position Sizing

Determine the right position size based on your risk tolerance.

Account Size Risk per Trade Max Position
$10,000 1% $100
$10,000 2% $200

Stop Loss Strategy

Protect your capital with proper stop loss placement.

  • Place stops below support for longs
  • Place stops above resistance for shorts
  • Consider volatility when setting stops

Take Profit Targets

Set realistic profit targets based on market structure.

  • Use risk:reward ratio of at least 1:2
  • Multiple targets for partial profits
  • Consider major resistance levels
