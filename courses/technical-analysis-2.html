Technical Analysis - Advanced Patterns & Indicators

2. Advanced Technical Indicators

RSI (Relative Strength Index)

Measures momentum by comparing the magnitude of recent gains to recent losses.

RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
RS = Average Gain / Average Loss
  • Above 70: Overbought
  • Below 30: Oversold

MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)

Shows the relationship between two moving averages of an asset's price.

MACD = 12-day EMA - 26-day EMA
Signal Line = 9-day EMA of MACD
  • MACD crosses above signal line: Bullish
  • MACD crosses below signal line: Bearish

Bollinger Bands

Shows volatility and potential overbought/oversold conditions.

Middle Band = 20-day SMA
Upper Band = Middle Band + (2 × σ)
Lower Band = Middle Band - (2 × σ)
  • Price at upper band: Potential resistance
  • Price at lower band: Potential support

Indicator Combinations

Using multiple indicators together can provide stronger trading signals:

  • RSI + MACD for momentum confirmation
  • Bollinger Bands + RSI for volatility and momentum
  • Moving Averages + MACD for trend confirmation
