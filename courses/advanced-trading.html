Understanding the order book can reveal potential support and resistance levels based on large orders.

Complexity:
High

Volume Profile Analysis

Volume profile helps identify key price levels where most trading activity occurs.

  • Point of Control (POC) - Price level with highest trading volume
  • Value Area - Range where 70% of trading occurs
  • Volume gaps can indicate potential breakout levels
