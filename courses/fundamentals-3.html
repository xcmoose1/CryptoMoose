Crypto Fundamentals - Buying & Trading Crypto

3. How to Buy and Trade Cryptocurrency

Centralized Exchanges (CEX)

Traditional exchange platforms with order books.

Popular Options:

  • Binance
  • Coinbase
  • Kraken

Pros: Easy to use, high liquidity

Cons: Requires KYC, custodial

Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)

Peer-to-peer trading through smart contracts.

Popular Options:

  • Uniswap
  • PancakeSwap
  • dYdX

Pros: No KYC, self-custody

Cons: Higher fees, more complex

Fee Comparison

Step-by-Step Guide to Buying Crypto

1

Choose an Exchange

Select based on your location, preferred payment method, and security needs.

2

Complete Verification

Prepare ID documents and complete KYC if using a centralized exchange.

3

Add Funds

Deposit using bank transfer, credit card, or existing crypto.

4

Place Order

Choose between market order (instant) or limit order (specific price).

5

Secure Storage

Transfer to your wallet for safe storage.
