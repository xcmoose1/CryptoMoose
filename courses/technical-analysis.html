1. Understanding Chart Patterns

Chart patterns are the foundation of technical analysis. They represent the psychology of market participants and can help predict future price movements.

Essential Chart Patterns:

  • Support and Resistance Levels
  • Trend Lines and Channels
  • Double Tops and Bottoms
  • Head and Shoulders Pattern
  • Bull and Bear Flags
